HTML Worksheet

Throughout the weeks, we learned HTML skills with worksheets. Without them, this website won't exist.

(Click on the images to view the final product)

This site is the final product of worksheets 2 and 3. In this, we learn how to create a website with background colour, different text sizes, colour and alignment. We also learned about how to create a list, place images and link different pages together through a hyperlink. We also learned about flexbox. Using it, elements can fit any screen size because they will warp and flex according to the screen.
