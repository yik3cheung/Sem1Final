CARP Task

The following three works use the CARP design principles (contrast, repetition, alignment and proximity) we learned in week 3 to redesign the poster for the Bristol International Balloon Fiesta. With one extra poster as an improved version of one of the original three.

Inspired by the grid of a hot air balloon, this poster uses the grid to place images related to the event. The colourful gradient texts and logos contrast with the dark background, creating a striking image that catches viewers' attention.

Inspired by old prints, this poster uses text as the background with a hot air balloon in front. The bottom includes all the information and sponsors, increasing readability. The palette, font and filters of this poster resemble an old newspaper or print that has been yellowed because of age.
